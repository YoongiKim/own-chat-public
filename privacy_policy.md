# Privacy Policy for OwnChat

**Effective Date: March 11, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains the data practices of **OwnChat** ("we," "us," or "our") when you use our mobile application (the "App"). 

Our core philosophy is absolute privacy. We do not have servers, and **we do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data.**

## 1. Zero Data Collection

- **No Accounts:** OwnChat does not require you to create an account, nor does it collect user names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
- **No Analytics or Trackers:** We do not collect device information, usage data, crash logs, or any tracking analytics.
- **No Cloud Storage by Us:** We do not collect or monitor your messages, attachments, or any content you create.

## 2. API Keys & AI Providers

OwnChat acts as a secure, local wrapper for connecting to third-party AI services (such as OpenAI's ChatGPT, Google's Gemini, or Anthropic's Claude). 

- **Local Storage:** The API keys you enter are securely stored **only on your device** (using secure storage mechanisms). They are never sent to us or any third-party analytics service.
- **Direct Communication:** When you send a message, your data and API keys are transmitted directly from your device to the respective AI provider's official API servers. We do not route your traffic through any intermediate servers.
- **Third-Party Policies:** Because your data is sent directly to the AI providers you choose to use, we encourage you to review their respective privacy policies to understand how they handle your prompts and data:
  - [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
  - [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  - [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy)

## 3. Data Stored on Your Device

All of your chat history, settings, and media files are stored locally in the App's database on your physical device. **You have complete control over this data.** If you delete a chat or uninstall the App, that data is permanently removed from your device.

## 4. Children's Privacy

Because our App does not collect any personal data, it does not collect information from children under the age of 13. However, users should be aware of the age restrictions imposed by the third-party AI providers they connect to.

## 5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
